 Project Title: CSE – Campus Stock Exchange
Description:
CSE is an interactive web platform that transforms everyday college life into a dynamic virtual economy. In this system, each participating student is represented as a tradable “stock,” whose value fluctuates based on real-world events, peer perception, and market activity. Users can buy and sell these stocks, build portfolios, and earn virtual currency through strategic decision-making.
The platform integrates three interconnected modules:
1. Stock Market Dashboard – Students trade virtual stocks representing their peers. Stock prices are influenced by demand-supply mechanics, verified campus events, and community-driven news, creating a realistic and engaging market simulation.
2. Event Prediction Market – Users can place bets on upcoming college events (such as sports matches or competitions). Outcomes of these events directly impact both user earnings and stock price movements, linking real-world activities with the virtual economy.
3. Auction-Based Dating System – Users can utilize their earned virtual currency to participate in a consent-based auction dating system. Participants can bid to express interest, but final interaction is always based on mutual selection, ensuring fairness, control, and respect while maintaining a unique and engaging user experience.
A key feature of the platform is its credibility-driven news system, where users can post updates or events that influence stock prices. These updates are validated through a weighted voting mechanism and user credibility scores, reducing misinformation and encouraging responsible participation.
By combining elements of financial markets, prediction systems, and auction-based dating, CSE offers a unique, satirical yet insightful simulation of how reputation, perception, and actions shape value in a community.
Key Highlights:
● Gamified stock market based on real social dynamics
● Integrated event betting and prediction system
● Consent-driven auction-based dating module
● Credibility-based news verification mechanism
● Unified virtual currency economy
This project demonstrates the intersection of technology, economics, and social behavior, delivering an engaging and thought-provoking experience within a college environment.
