 CampusEx Featurette:
User onboarding
- Verify user using college email id (ex lci2024035@iiitl.ac.in)
- Create user profile using name, profile picture(optional), stock symbol(ex KUB for
Kuber)
- Stock symbol can be randomly generated and must be unique, or user can choose
them
- There will be an initial balance of points/coins(viz. AURA coins) say 100 or 1000
- Each user will have a credibility score associated with them.
- Once a user creates his/her profile, he will automatically be listed onto the market,
the price can be default or decided by the admin
- While signing up, the user tells the platform about himself, like what are his traits,
qualities, hobbies, etc. let’s call them traits for now. Say, he gives 10 traits, so out of
those 10, random half of them will be hidden to the public
- Can create his IPO date, through which he will be launched into the market on that
particular date and users can preorder/pre purchase his stock before hand which could determine their price on the launch date based on pre purchases
Stock Market
- Using these aura coins, users can purchase stocks of someone listed in the market.
- The price dynamics works just as a real stock market, viz. major buying could
increase the price and selling could decrease it.
- Just about the dynamic pricing is that, the price won’t update immediately, it will only
update just an hour before the market opens, and will stay the same through-out the
day.
- The market will be open only from 9am to 5pm from monday to friday, like the real
stock market timings.
- The user can view their portfolio on some page, where he sees his purchased stocks,
and their current values and trends
- There will be a graph for each stock, not candlestick, but basic curvy lines
- There will be a top10/top50 leaderboard of top stocks, like nifty50
Bidding
Profile Bidding
- About the bidding feature, now bidding is very random.
- Each day for a specific amount of time, someone’s profile will be bidded for
- NOTE: The profile of that person will be bidded, and not the stock
- Now, the bidders will be able to see the current highest bid amount
- A user is allowed to put multiple bids, but one single message on the first bid, will be
attached to his profile during the bidding period for that bid
 
 - With each user’s bid, there will be a bid message associated with it
- The one who is being bid at (for example X), so X will be able to see all the bidders,
their profiles, their bid messages, their bid amounts
- Now after the bidding is complete, then X can see the list of people in ranking, with
their names, bid amounts, and messages in a tier list
- And X will have the option to accept or reject the topmost bidder.
- If X rejects the topmost bidder, his card will vanish, and rankings will shift
- Now again X will have the option to accept or reject the topmost bidder
- X can reject at most 3 times
- If X uses all her 3 rejections, then the next bidder will automatically be accepted and
be asked to transfer the amount.
Event Bidding
- Now college hosts a lot of events, say sports, tech events etc.
- One particular bidder can bid only once on a particular profile for that window.
- Here the admin will come into action and create bid events for a limited amount of
time, specific to the ongoing real life event
- Consider sports, some badminton tournament is being held at college
- Now in the event bids section, users can see the tournament being created to bid,
and the players on the stake.
- Users will bid for or against players, or try to predict outcomes of the
game/tournament.
- Rewards (Aura coins) will be awarded accordingly, depending upon the real event
outcome.
Dating
- Now, after profile bidding, when the bidder is accepted and after the bidder buys the profile
- He can see the complete profile of the person, including the hidden traits of the person.
- Chat option will get unlocked for those two people
- Both the users can then rate each other based on their experience, which will affect
their stock price
- They can gift each other their Aura coins
News
- For the news feature, if anything happens in the college, and someone publishes it on the platform
- A voting will be set up for confirming the validity of the news.
- Voting will have two options viz. Can confirm and cannot confirm
- Voting will be weighted, ie more weightage will be given to the person’s vote, who’s
credibility is high

 - After a specific amount of time, the voting will be closed and results will affect the stock prices of the publisher and voters
- If the news turned out to be true, then the credibility score of the publisher will increase, along with his stock price
- The confirmators of the news will also get a bump in their credibility score, whereas the non confirmators’ credibility score reduces.
- Now the subject of the news, whoever it may concern, upon the confirmation of the news, the stock price of that person will get affected, positive or negative.
Power shop
- This is a shop where you can spend your aura coins, to get perks or power ups(like a game)
- Now the perks and power ups can vary in price, rarity and purpose
Admin Control
- Admin is the god tier of the platform with infinite aura points
- Admin login access will be only with us, to implement, test, upgrade, add, remove
features etc.
- Can create bid events at any point of time, for any duration of time.(Be it profile bids,
or event bids)
- Can be able to change/override stock prices
- Can be able to donate aura coins to anyone
- Can change credibility score
- Can temporarily/permanently ban someone from the platform
- Can add items in the power shop, and define their purpose/usecase logically.
